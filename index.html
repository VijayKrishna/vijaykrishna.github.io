Research & Academics ››

I am currently a PhD student in the Software Engineering program, at the Department of Informatics, University of California, Irvine. I hold a degree in B.E. in Computers from the University of Pune, India.

As a student of Software Engineering Research, I am interested in building and studying reusable models of the runtime behavior of software components that can be leveraged for a number of software development, maintenance, and testing tasks.

I work with Prof. James A. Jones in the Spider Lab. James' research is focused towards software testing, analysis (compile-time and run-time) and debugging.
